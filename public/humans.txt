This website for the Maryland Institute for Technology 
in the Humanities has been developed at the University
of Maryland since 1999. Over that time it has had many
curators, who have used a variety of web publishing tools. 
Please visit our archive to see what some of these 
projects have looked like: https://archive.mith.umd.edu
and take a look at our staff page to see who some of
these people are: https://mith.umd.edu/people/
